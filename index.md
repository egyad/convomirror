# Privacy Policy for Convo Mirror

**Last Updated: January 11, 2026**

## Introduction

Convo Mirror ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

---

## Information We Collect

### Photos and Screenshots

**What We Collect:**
- Conversation screenshots that you voluntarily upload to the app for analysis

**How We Use It:**
- Screenshots are sent to Google's Gemini AI API for relationship analysis
- Images are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers
- Analysis results are stored locally on your device only

**Data Retention:**
- Screenshots are transmitted to Gemini AI for processing only
- We do not store your screenshots on our servers
- All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
- You can delete your local data at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking purposes
- Browsing history
- Contact lists
- Any other personal information

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the screenshots you provide solely for:

1. **AI Analysis**: Sending images to Google's Gemini AI API to generate relationship insights
2. **App Functionality**: Displaying analysis results within the app
3. **Local Storage**: Saving your analysis history on your device for your reference

We do NOT use your information for:
- Marketing or advertising
- Selling to third parties
- Tracking across apps or websites
- Any purpose other than providing the core app functionality

### No Other Third Parties

We do not share your data with any other third-party services, analytics providers, or advertisers.

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- All analysis results and history are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
- Data is not synchronized to cloud services
- Data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app

### Data Transmission Security

- All data transmitted to Google's Gemini AI is sent over secure HTTPS connections
- We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit

### No Server-Side Storage

- We do not operate servers that store your personal data
- We do not maintain databases of user information
- We do not create user accounts or profiles

---

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control

You have complete control over your data:

- **View**: Access your analysis history within the app
- **Delete**: Remove individual analyses from your history
- **Clear All**: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
- **Opt-Out**: Stop using the app at any time

### Data Deletion

To delete your data:

1. **Delete Individual Analyses**: Tap the delete icon on any history item
2. **Clear All App Data**: 
   - iOS: Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Convo Mirror → Delete App
   - Android: Settings → Apps → Convo Mirror → Storage → Clear Data
3. **Uninstall**: Remove the app from your device
